Programming and Software Development

Here at the Programming and software development lab, PS for short, we are learning a lot of things, and not only how to do these things, but HOW the work, and WHY they work. As the year goes on, we learn even more, and it's not always from the teacher (pictured below), but sometimes even our peers. A few of the things we've learned/are learning are:

  • C#
  • Excel
  • HTML
  • Design
  • JavaScript

Appropriate work environment

The lab is set up to be a proper work environment to prepare you for the work force, and it certainly feels that way as well, with breaks, meetings, and frequent personal work time, we come in each day eager to either learn something new, or to work on our own projects.
