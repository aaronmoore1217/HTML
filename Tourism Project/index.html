Discover Columbus

A vibrant city combining nature, culture, and history. Explore districts like Short North and German Village. Immerse yourself in charmming parks and the scenic Scioto Mile. Enjoy the warm and welcoming atmosphere of Columbus, that will make you want to return for more.


Uber/Lyft/etc.

Uber is a convenient and efficient alternative to traditional taxis. With just a few taps on your smartphone, you can easily hail an Uber, saving you the hassle of flagging down a taxi on the street. While it may not always be the most affordable option, Uber offers a reliable way to reach your destination precisely. Additionally, Uber drivers strive to provide a clean and comfortable environment, ensuring that you have a pleasant ride every time.

COTA

COTA offers a convenient and affordable transportation option for commuters. With a monthly pass priced at just 61 dollars, it stands as the second cheapest choice available. While it may require a short walk to reach your destination, it does bring you in close proximity. Though not the cleanest ride, COTA reliably takes you where you need to go. It strikes a perfect balance between the cost-effective option of walking and the convenience of taking an Uber.

Walking/Bike

Walking and biking offer affordable and convenient means of transportation, albeit at a slower pace. These modes of travel come with a price tag of absolutely nothing, making them the cheapest options available. Not only do they help you reach your destination, but they also contribute to maintaining good health. Both walking and biking can be quite relaxing, allowing you to enjoy the scenery and unwind. However, biking provides an added benefit of a more vigorous workout, making it a faster and healthier alternative to walking. So whether you choose to walk or bike, you'll be able to save money, stay active, and enjoy the therapeutic aspects of these activities.
