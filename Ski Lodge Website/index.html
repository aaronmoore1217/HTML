About Us

Snow Fever Ski and Board School is a luxurious resort nestled in the mountains, offering breathtaking views and world-class amenities. Whether you're looking for a relaxing getaway or an experience to learn from, we have something for everyone.

Our Rooms

Our rooms are designed to provide the ultimate comfort and relaxation during your stay. We offer a range of options, including standard rooms, suites, and cabins.

  • Standard Room - Starting at $150 per night
  • Suite - Starting at $250 per night
  • Cabin - Starting at $350 per night

Activities

At Snow Fever Ski and Board School, we offer a variety of activities to suit every interest:

  • Skiing and snowboarding
  • Snowshoeing and cross-country skiing
  • Sleigh rides
  • Ice skating
  • Indoor pool and hot tub
  • Spa services
