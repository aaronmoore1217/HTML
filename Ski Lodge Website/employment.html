Park Ranger

The park ranger patrols campgrounds, trails, and the surrounding area to ensure the safety and happiness of park visitors and the natural enviorment. You should be passionate about the conservation of the natural enviorment, as well as dedicated to ensuring the safety of visitors.

    Responsibilities and Requirements

  • Explaining park rules and safety regulations.
  • Performing park maintenance.
  • Performing emergency care, such as CPR, first aid, search and rescue, and assisting with stopping or containing forest fires.

  • Bachelor's degree in forestry, conservation science, environmental science, or related field.
  • First aid and CPR certified
  • Physically fit
  • Strong communication skills

Trail Guide

Guides will lead tours throughout a series of trails, as well as teach about the history of the park. Inn-based tours and Camping-based tours lead to a wide arrangement of hikes. A love for nature and an ability to lead are must have for this position.

    Responsibilities and Requirements

  • Leadership and safety of guests on hikes.
  • Ability to learn and teach guests the history the park.
  • Be a counsleor to the guests in your tour / help guests through mood changes and anxiety.
  • Long trips require you make meals for the guests in your tours.

  • Must have WFR certification
  • Must be able to drive
  • Must have skills in food prep and cooking
